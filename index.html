📱 About CamLingo

CamLingo is an innovative language learning app that uses Google Gemini AI to help you learn vocabulary through photo recognition. Turn any photo into a learning opportunity!

Key Features:

  • Photo Album Recognition: Select images from your photo library and let AI identify objects to learn related vocabulary
  • Camera Recognition: Take photos in real-time and instantly get vocabulary learning content
  • Local Storage: Save your photos and learning records on your device for offline review
  • 17 Language Support: Interface and vocabulary learning in 17 languages
  • Personalized Learning: Set your learning language and native language preferences
  • Offline Review: Access your saved photos and vocabulary anytime
Privacy Notice: Since photos are uploaded to Google Gemini for recognition, please do not take or upload photos containing personal privacy information such as documents, ID cards, credit cards, or other sensitive personal data.
Usage Limits: Free users receive 3 complimentary photo recognition attempts. Each recognition counts as one attempt, including re-recognizing the same photo or switching languages. Changing your learning language or native language requires new recognition and consumes additional attempts.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

After downloading the app, you'll be guided to select your learning language and native language. Once setup is complete, tap "Photo Library" or "Camera" to start learning vocabulary through photo recognition.

Why does the app need camera and photo permissions?

The app requires these permissions to:

  • Photo Library: Let you select existing photos for vocabulary recognition
  • Camera: Enable real-time photo capture for instant learning
  • These permissions are only used when you actively choose to use these features

How are recognition attempts counted?

Each successful photo recognition uses one attempt:

  • Recognizing the same photo multiple times = multiple attempts
  • Changing learning language and re-recognizing = additional attempts
  • Free users have 3 total attempts
  • Recognition only works for your currently selected learning and native languages

What photos should I avoid uploading?

For your privacy and security, please do not upload photos containing:

  • Personal documents (passports, driver's licenses, ID cards)
  • Financial information (credit cards, bank statements)
  • Medical records or prescriptions
  • Screenshots of personal messages or emails
  • Any other sensitive personal information

Remember: Photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for recognition, so only upload images of objects you want to learn vocabulary about.

What happens to my photos?

Your privacy is important to us:

  • Photos are only sent to Google Gemini API for recognition purposes
  • We do not upload or store any user information on our servers
  • Your photos and learning records are saved locally on your device only
  • You have full control over your data

Can I use the app offline?

Partially - you can review your saved photos and vocabulary offline, but photo recognition requires an internet connection to access the Google Gemini AI service.

How accurate is the AI recognition?

We use Google Gemini, one of the most advanced AI models available. Recognition accuracy depends on photo quality, lighting, and object clarity. For best results, ensure good lighting and clear object visibility.
