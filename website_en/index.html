Frequently Asked Questions

How does CamLingo work?

Our app combines optical character recognition (OCR) with advanced AI language models to detect text in images and translate it instantly.

Which languages are supported?

CamLingo currently supports Chinese (Simplified), Chinese (Traditional), English (US & UK), Spanish (Spain & Mexico), Portuguese (Portugal & Brazil), French (France & Canada), Japanese, Korean, German, Russian, Italian, Dutch, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, Turkish and Polish.

What is the difference between Free and Pro?

The Free plan lets you try all features with a limited number of image translations. Pro removes these limits and unlocks priority processing.

How is my data protected?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You may permanently delete your account and associated data at any time from Settings.
